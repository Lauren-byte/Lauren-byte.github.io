About Me

Lauren Stevens

Lauren Stevens is an aspiring filmmaker who dreams of working in film production. Her goal is to one day work for a studio specializing in animated films, as she loves world building and the endless possibilities of the imagination. She found her love of animated film when she entered high school, and decided to devote the rest of her education to figuring out the part she wanted to play behind the scenes. Her accomplishments consist of learning the essence of video production as well as the concept of media entirely. She focuses on the more visually stunning aspects of film that create an immersive environment for the audience. She’s studied 2D and 3D design as well as photography and videography in order to create visually engaging videos and projects. She is now in her sophomore year at Howard Community College and her education so far has enabled her to learn the craft of film and cultivate her technique. She strives to learn more and expand upon her knowledge by learning from the many professionals in the industry, as well as the newer filmmakers similar to her situation. She plans to attend a four year college to get her degree in film and better prepare her for her introduction to the industry, beginning her journey towards her goal.
